Full Stack Developer

Upon completing my graduate studies in 2009 with a Master of Business Administration, I set out to create my own advertising agency (it’s good to dream big!). A few weeks after graduation, I got a call from a global consumer goods company, and like any other young grad, I couldn't refuse and I am glad I didn't refuse, my first job was the perfect combination of Marketing and Technology, it exposed a non-tech-savvy version of me to the compelling world of binary-digits (still mesmerized and can't comprehend how powerful computers are, with just two vocabs in their dictionary, i.e., 1 & 0). Gradually, the world of E-commerce and social media innovations crept up on me and when a childish thought of dabbling with codes metamorphosed into a juggernaut, which propelled a profound desire to let my inner developer take total control of me.

"Anything's possible if you've got enough nerve." - J. K. Rowling
So, to gratify my yearning to make a positive impact and creative something innovative, I gave up all and took this dive to restart my life as a developer, went back to college, started SMU Coding boot camp to unlearn, re-learn, create, build and inspire.
