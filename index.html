About Me

Upon completing my graduate studies in 2009 with a Master of business in Marketing, I set out to make it big and create my own Advertising agency (its good to dream big!). Few weeks after my graduation, I got a call from a global consumer goods company, and like many young grads, I couldn't refuse...


But I am glad I didn't, my first job was the perfect combination of Marketing and Technology, and it exposed a non-tech-savvy version of mine to the compelling world of binary-digits (still mesmerized and can't comprehend how powerful computers are, with just two vocab in their dictionary, i.e. 1 & 0);


Gradually, the world of E-commerce and Social media innovations crept up on me and propelled a profound desire to let my inner developer juggernaut to take the total control of me.


To gratify my yearnings and desires to make the positive impact I gave up all and took this dive to restart my life as a developer, To create, To build and to Inspire.
