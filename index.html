About Me

Upon completing my graduate studies in 2009 with a Master of Business in Marketing, I set out to create my own Advertising agency (it’s good to dream big!). Few weeks after my graduation, I got a call from a global consumer goods company, and like any other young grad, I couldn't refuse.


I am glad I didn't, my first job was the perfect combination of Marketing and Technology, and it exposed a non-tech-savvy version of mine to the compelling world of binary-digits (still mesmerized and can't comprehend how powerful computers are, with just two vocabs in their dictionary, i.e., 1 & 0).


Gradually, the world of E-Commerce and Social media innovations crept up on me and when a childish thought of dabbling with codes metamorphised into a juggernaut, which propelled a profound desire to let my inner developer take total control of me.

"Anything's possible if you've got enough nerve." - J. K. Rowling﻿

So, to gratify my yearnings and desires to make the positive impact I gave up all and took this dive to restart my life as a developer, Enrolled into a Coding boot camp to unlearn, re-learn, create, build and Inspire.

Feel free to slide into my mailbox, and I look forward to the notification "You've got a Mail!".
